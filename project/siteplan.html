Site Purpose

The Funval Institute website will serve as a comprehensive online platform for prospective and current students, offering detailed information about the institute's educational programs, courses, registration procedures, and contact details. It aims to be the primary digital touchpoint for inquiries, student support, and showcasing the institute's values and success stories. The site will facilitate easy access to course catalogs, admission requirements, and provide a clear pathway for enrollment.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Prospective Student Inquiry
    "I am interested in vocational training programs. Where can I find a list of all courses offered by Funval, their schedules, and tuition fees?"
  • Scenario 2: Current Student Support
    "I am a registered student and need to find information about my class schedule, academic calendar, or contact details for student support."
  • Scenario 3: Partnership and Collaboration
    "Our organization is looking for educational partners. How can we learn more about Funval's mission and explore potential collaborations?"

Color Schema

Our chosen color scheme is inspired by trust, professionalism, and vibrancy, aiming to create an inviting and credible online presence.

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue (#0056b3)

    Usage: Headings (H1, H2), navigation bar background, important buttons (e.g., "Enroll Now"), and key icons. Represents stability, trust, and professionalism.

  • Accent Color: Bright Orange (#fd7e14)

    Usage: Call-to-action buttons, highlights, emphasis on important information, and subtle accents in graphics. Represents energy, enthusiasm, and innovation.

  • Text Color: Dark Grey (#333333)

    Usage: All paragraph text and general content for readability.

  • Background Color: Light Grey (#f8f9fa)

    Usage: Main body background for a clean and modern look.

Typography

We've selected fonts that prioritize readability and a professional appearance while maintaining a friendly tone.

  • Headings (H1, H2, H3): 'Roboto' (Sans-serif)

    Example: Funval Institute - Learn, Grow, Succeed.

    Usage: Provides a strong, modern, and easily scannable look for titles and section headers.

  • Body Text & Paragraphs: 'Open Sans' (Sans-serif)

    Example: "Our mission is to empower individuals through quality education and practical skills."

    Usage: Chosen for its excellent readability across various screen sizes, making content accessible and pleasant to read.
